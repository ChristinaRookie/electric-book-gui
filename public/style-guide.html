Review submitted changes

This is shown after the user hits "Review submitted changes (x)" in the header: it's a list of PRs. The title and h1 of the page match the link text that they just clicked, so that they know they're in the right place. "To review" is open PRs; "Reviewed" is closed PRs. The links in each block show the details of the PR. The li gets submission-accepted or submission-rejected depending on the state of the PR.
